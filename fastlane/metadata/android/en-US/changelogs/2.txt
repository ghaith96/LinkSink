v1.1.0 - Topics, Search & Metadata

New Features:
• Topics - Organize links into topics with custom names
• Per-topic webhooks - LOCAL_ONLY, global, or custom webhook per topic
• Fuzzy search - Search links by URL or title with typo tolerance
• Date filtering - Filter links by Today, This Week, or This Month
• Link metadata - Automatically fetches title, description, and thumbnail
• Topic management - Create, edit, and delete topics from Settings

Improvements:
• Enhanced share sheet with quick topic selection
• Filter chips show active filters at a glance
• Links display their assigned topic
• Better offline handling with queued sync
• Improved search works reliably on all devices
