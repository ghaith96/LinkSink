LinkSink is a simple, privacy-focused app for saving and organizing links from any app on your device.

Features:
• Share links directly from any app using Android's share menu
• Automatically syncs saved links to Discord via webhooks
• Local-first architecture - your data stays on your device
• Works offline with background sync when connected
• Material You theming that adapts to your system colors
• Clean, minimal interface designed for quick link capture

LinkSink is free and open source software. No accounts required, no tracking, no ads.

Perfect for:
• Saving articles to read later
• Collecting research links
• Sharing discoveries with Discord communities
• Building a personal link archive
